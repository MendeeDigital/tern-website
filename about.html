We're TERN.

And we believe in you.

Since 2016, we've been walking alongside refugees as they discover that their ideas, their skills, and their determination can change everything.

It started with a simple question

What if the experience of being a refugee — the resilience, the creativity, the sheer determination to rebuild — wasn't just something to survive, but something to build on?

That question became TERN. Since 2016, we've supported over 1,500 entrepreneurs from 65 nationalities. Not with pity, but with respect. Not with handouts, but with tools, mentorship, and a community that says: "You've got this, and we've got you."

What we hold close

These aren't corporate values on a wall. They're promises we make to every person who walks through our door.

Every idea matters

No dream is too small. Whether it's a food stall or a tech startup, we treat every idea with the same respect and excitement.

We walk together

This isn't a programme you go through alone. It's a community where people share meals, swap stories, and lift each other up.

Doors should be open

We work to change systems so that talent — not background — determines opportunity. Because everyone deserves a fair chance.
