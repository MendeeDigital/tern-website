About TERN

The Entrepreneurial Refugee Network C.I.C.

Enabling refugees to thrive through the power of their own ideas.

Who We Are

TERN is a social enterprise and nonprofit that helps refugees become entrepreneurs, launch businesses, and take control of their own futures. Since 2016, we have supported over 1,500 entrepreneurs from 65 nationalities.

We believe that refugees bring incredible skills, determination, and creativity. Our role is to provide the support, resources, and community they need to turn their ideas into thriving businesses.

What We Believe

Power of Ideas

Every person has the potential to create something meaningful. We help refugees harness their ideas and entrepreneurial spirit.

Community First

We build supportive communities where entrepreneurs can learn from each other, share experiences, and grow together.

Systemic Change

We work to change systems and structures so that refugee entrepreneurs can access the opportunities they deserve.
