You belong here

You have an idea.
We'll help you make it real.

Starting a business in a new country can feel overwhelming. That's okay. Hundreds of people just like you have been where you are now — and they made it. You can too. Here's how we'll walk this path together:

Step 1

Share your dream with us

You don't need a business plan. You don't need perfect English. You just need an idea — even a small one. Our Spark programme is a safe, friendly space to explore what's possible. We'll listen, and we'll help you figure out the next step.

"I came to TERN with just a thought. They made me feel like it was worth something."
Step 2

Meet someone who gets it

We'll match you with a mentor — someone in your industry who genuinely cares. They'll meet with you regularly, answer your questions, and help you navigate the things that feel confusing. You're not alone in this.

"My mentor didn't just give advice — she believed in me before I believed in myself."
Step 3

Build it, your way

Workshops, funding guidance, practical tools — all free. We'll help you with the things that feel hard: registering a business, finding money, building a website, reaching customers. Step by step, at your pace.

"They helped me get my first grant. I cried. It was the first time someone invested in my future."
Step 4

You're part of a family now

500+ entrepreneurs from 65 countries — people who understand your journey because they've lived it. Events, peer support, and friendships that last. TERN isn't just a programme. It's a community that has your back.

"I found people who feel like home. We celebrate each other's wins like our own."

Ready? We'd love to meet you.

It's free. There's no commitment. Just a conversation about your idea and how we can help.
